PRICING

Our pricing is based on the size of the object you want printed. The larger the object, the more material is used, and the longer it takes to print. The price is also based on the quality of the print. The higher the quality, the more time and detail is put into the print. All order requests will be assumed default print settings unless requested for higher quality.

Pricing Details:

  • Base price: $2.99 for every order up to 50 grams of filament.
  • For orders over 50 grams, an additional $0.05 is added per gram beyond 50 grams.
  • If you request higher build quality, the final price is multiplied by 1.5x.

For a more accurate price, please contact us directly.
